About The property

Beautiful three bedroom, three bath luxurious waterfront townhome on Clearwater Beach. This three-story townhome features an oversized two car garage and an elevator that goes to all three levels including a rooftop sitting area for you to enjoy long summer days. This townhome is in a great location on Clearwater Harbor close to all the fantastic beaches, shops, restaurants, and nightlife Clearwater Beach has to offer. Enjoy the sea life and gentle Florida breezes from the large rooftop or the balcony that is located off the living room. Beautiful water and pool views, along with expansive views of downtown Clearwater to the east and the newly reinvented Clearwater Beach to the west. If you're into boating this townhome comes with its own private boat slip with a lift and has water deep enough for a sailboat. The building was painted this year and the roof was redone a year and a half ago. This is a well-maintained townhome and a must see! The Sellers will consider selling furniture for their convenience.
